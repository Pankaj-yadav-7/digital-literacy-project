# Social Media Do's and Don'ts 

## Do's
- Think before posting anything online.
- Think for 60 seconds before posting something emotional.
- Fact-check information before sharing it on your feed.
- Network with alumni and professionals in your field.
- Keep your personal information safe.

## Don'ts
- Don't share fake news or rumors.
- Don't engage in "flaming" or public arguments with peers.
- Don't post confidential information about your school or workplace.
- Don't use excessive slang or offensive language.
- Don't post content that displays illegal activities or policy violations.
